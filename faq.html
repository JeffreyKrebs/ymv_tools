Photo Preparation & Album Policies

Why must I remove photos from albums myself?

  • Scanner Safety: Our equipment can only scan loose, individual photos.
  • Aging Adhesives: Many older albums use glue that becomes very strong over time.
  • Preventing Damage: Removing photos can cause tearing or cracking, and we want you to stay in control of that delicate step.

What sizes and shapes of photos can you scan?

  • Most Shapes Welcome: We can scan square, rectangular, and panoramic prints.
  • Minimum Size: 3.5 × 5 inches (89 × 127 mm)
  • Maximum Size: 8.5 × 11 inches (203 × 254 mm)

How should I organize my loose photos?

  • Group Them Physically: Sort your photos into piles or envelopes.
  • Digital Mirroring: Each group becomes its own digital folder or online album.
  • Label Clearly: Write the folder name on each envelope (e.g., “Family Trip 1998”).

Pricing, Extras, & Custom Services

Is scanning the back of a photo included?

  • Front Only: Standard pricing covers the front image.
  • Back Text Available: If you want handwritten notes scanned, there is a small per‑photo fee.
  • Kept Together: Front and back images are delivered side‑by‑side in your digital folder.

Do you fix damaged or faded photos?

  • Basic Enhancement Included: Every scan gets automatic color and exposure balancing.
  • Full Restoration Available: Tears, stains, and heavy fading can be repaired for an additional fee.
  • Custom Quote: Restoration varies by photo, so we provide pricing individually.

Delivery, Timeline, & Payments

How long does the scanning process take?

  • Standard Turnaround: Most orders are completed within 7 business days.
  • Larger Projects: Big collections or restoration work may take a little longer.

What payment methods do you accept?

  • e‑Transfer: Fast and secure.
  • Cash: Accepted if you prefer paying in person.

Do I get my physical photos back?

  • Absolutely: All originals, envelopes, and groupings are returned exactly as received
